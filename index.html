Introduction to Social Studies

Social Studies is the study of human society, its development, structure, and functioning. It combines history, geography, political science, economics, and culture to help us understand the world we live in. By studying social sciences, we learn about the past, analyze the present, and prepare for the future.

Economics

Economics explains how individuals, businesses, and nations use resources. It covers trade, money, industries, and development. By understanding economics, we learn how wealth is created and shared.

  • Microeconomics: Focuses on individual choices and businesses.
  • Macroeconomics: Studies national income, inflation, unemployment, policies.
  • Global Economy: International trade, globalization, and economic relations.
